Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 12, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how MyFinance collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application. MyFinance is an expense budget tracker system designed to help you manage your personal finances by tracking your income, expenses, and budget.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services to you. The data we collect includes:

  • Personal Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a username. This information is used for authentication and to personalize your experience.
  • Financial Data: We store the financial data you input, including transaction amounts, categories (e.g., Food, Transportation), descriptions, and dates. This data is the core of our service and is used solely to provide you with financial insights and analytics.
  • Usage Data: We may collect data on how you interact with the app, such as features used and time spent. This helps us improve app performance and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and maintain our service, including allowing you to track your budget and expenses.
  • Analyze usage to improve our application's features and functionality.
  • Communicate with you regarding your account, updates, or support inquiries.

Note: Your financial data is used exclusively for internal analytics to help you manage your finances and for us to monitor overall user trends to improve the app. We do not share your individual financial data with any third parties.

3. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your data. Your information is stored securely using Firebase Firestore, a cloud-based NoSQL database. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of your data.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information and financial data at any time through the application settings.
